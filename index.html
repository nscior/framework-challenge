Tap and Tune

Tap!

Use this part of the app to find any tempo to any song! Just start tapping the screen to the beat, and the number on the screen will be the average tempo of the song! Use this part of the app to find the BPM of a song instead of trying to count or search online, it's much easier to do!

What is Tap and Tune?

Tap and Tune is a new app designed for tablets to help composers with multiple issues when making music or using instruments.

Tune!

Use this part of the app to tune your instruments! Using the internal microphone on the tablet, we can help guide you to the correct tuning by using the top wheel as a guide. Just aim the center circle in the arc and your instrument will sound perfectly tuned! The note tuned to, cents for accuracy, and tuning numbers are included to accomodate for different instruments and to make it precise.
